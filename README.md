## Problem Statement

India has massive rooftop solar potential — but **80% of it goes untapped**.

Not because people don't want solar, but because:

- No one knows exactly how much energy their specific rooftop can generate — generic calculators are wildly inaccurate
- Banks and NBFCs reject solar loans because there's no reliable energy yield proof
- Installers give inflated ROI estimates, eroding consumer trust
- Slum clusters, small factories, and Tier-3 towns are completely ignored by existing tools

> The gap isn't technology. It's hyperlocal, trustworthy, actionable intelligence.

---


## Our Solution — GreenLens

GreenLens is a **hyperlocal solar energy intelligence platform** that provides precise, 
site-specific solar yield estimates for any rooftop in India — generating reports that 
homeowners, lenders, and installers can actually trust and act on.

Unlike generic calculators that give the same answer to everyone in a state, GreenLens 
treats every rooftop as unique. A 50m² roof in Patia, Bhubaneswar gets a completely 
different prediction than a 50m² roof in Cuttack — because the irradiance, dust levels, 
shading patterns, and local electricity tariffs are different. That specificity is what 
makes GreenLens useful in the real world.

---

### The Core Problem We Solve Differently

Most existing tools answer one question: *"How much solar can I generate?"*

GreenLens answers five questions in one flow:
1. **How much can my specific rooftop generate?** — Using real NASA satellite data for your exact GPS location
2. **How much money will I actually save?** — Using your city's actual DISCOM electricity tariff
3. **What government subsidy do I qualify for?** — Auto-calculated from PM Surya Ghar rules
4. **Who should I call to install it?** — MNRE-verified, rated, local installers near you
5. **Can I get a bank loan for this?** — Yes, because we generate a bankable PDF certificate no other tool provides
