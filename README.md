## 🚨 The Problem

India's rooftop solar revolution is stalled — not by a lack of technology, but by a critical gap in **trustworthy, hyperlocal intelligence**.

| Pain Point | Impact |
|---|---|
| 🔍 Generic solar calculators | Wildly inaccurate estimates; no site-specific data |
| 🏦 Loan rejections | Banks & NBFCs have no reliable energy yield proof |
| 📈 Inflated installer ROI claims | Erodes consumer trust & slows adoption |
| 🏘️ Ignored segments | Slums, small factories, Tier-3 towns — completely left out |

> **The gap isn't technology. It's hyperlocal, trustworthy, actionable intelligence.**

---

## 💡 Our Solution — RoofWatt

RoofWatt delivers **precision solar intelligence** tailored to every rooftop — from a Mumbai chawl to a Chhattisgarh factory shed.

### What We Do
- 📡 **Hyperlocal energy yield estimation** — using satellite imagery, tilt/shadow analysis, and microclimate data
- 📄 **Bankable energy reports** — structured proof of yield for loan applications
- 🤝 **Installer accountability layer** — cross-verify installer quotes with our independent projections
- 🌍 **Inclusive by design** — built for Tier-2/3 towns, slum clusters, SME factories
