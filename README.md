# Problem Statement
>India has massive rooftop solar potential — but 80% of it goes untapped. Not because people don't want solar, but because:
- No one knows exactly how much energy their specific rooftop can generate (generic calculators are wildly inaccurate)
- Banks and NBFCs reject solar loans because there's no reliable energy yield proof
- Installers give inflated ROI estimates, eroding trust
- Slum clusters, small factories, and tier-3 towns are completely ignored by existing tools
- The gap isn't technology. It's hyperlocal, trustworthy, actionable intelligence.
